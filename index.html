About The Conference

Dissatisfaction and Problems always leads to innovation. Thus, Innovation is increasingly being seen as the currency of 21st century. The NCETTSEME 2023 provides a common platform to all the industry experts, academicians, research scholars and students to express their views and share their expertise knowledge about innovation. Also in this highly challenging economic scenario, the future prosperity of India is solely dependent on innovation, change and creativity.
